Student Attendance Management System

A digital platform designed to simplify attendance tracking, improve accuracy, and provide transparent access to attendance records for both administrators and students.

Admin Panel

Administrators manage student profiles and record daily attendance efficiently using a centralized system.

  • Add, update, and delete student records
  • Mark and update daily attendance
  • View attendance reports

Student Access

Students can securely log in and monitor their attendance records without manual paperwork.

  • View personal attendance history
  • Check monthly attendance summary
  • Access records anytime

This system improves efficiency, reduces errors, and ensures reliable attendance management in academic institutions.
